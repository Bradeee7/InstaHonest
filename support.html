Common Topics

  • Account & Sign-In: Issues signing in with Apple, linking with a partner, or managing your account.
  • Screen Time Permissions: Help setting up or troubleshooting Screen Time and Family Controls access.
  • Subscriptions & Billing: Questions about your subscription are handled by Apple. You can manage your subscription in Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions on your device.
  • Notifications: Not receiving alerts? Make sure notifications are enabled for InstaHonest in your device settings.
  • Account Deletion: You can delete your account and all associated data from within the app under Settings.

Bug Reports

Found a bug? Please email us with a description of the issue, what you expected to happen, and the device and iOS version you're using. Screenshots are always helpful.
